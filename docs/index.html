Welcome to Plural Collective

Plural Collective is a sweet shop in the Sellwood neighborhood housing 9 local businesses featuring leather, coffee, plants, jewelry, kids clothes, bath and body products, pottery, cutting boards and ethically sourced goods from around the globe. Some of our products are made on site in our makers studios, and we offer monthly workshops in a variety of mediums. A perfect stop to shop local handmade for any member of the family.
